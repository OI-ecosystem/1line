What it does

The pressure underneath the decision.

Difficult decisions rarely stall because you lack information — they stall because competing fears, identities and incentives pull you in opposite directions at the same time.

1LINE Decide reads the language you use to describe the decision and surfaces what's actually driving it: the stay path, the change path, the hidden driver underneath, and what your own words reveal about where you already lean. Not advice — a structured read of the pressure.

Example input

“I have been in a stable relationship for years, but recently I met someone who made me question whether I have been settling into comfort instead of living honestly. I do not want to hurt anyone, but I also do not want to ignore what this is revealing about my life and who I am becoming.”
