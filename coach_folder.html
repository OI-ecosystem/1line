What it does

Not motivation. Precision.

1LINE Coach is for the moments when life stops feeling aligned — when you're stuck, uncertain, or quietly conflicted but can't name why.

Rather than generic motivation, it reads the language you use to describe your situation and surfaces the patterns, tensions, blind spots and possible next steps held inside it. You reflect; it interprets; you receive direction. Not therapy — something more precise.

Example input

“I'm considering leaving my job but I'm not sure whether I'm running toward something better or simply escaping.”
