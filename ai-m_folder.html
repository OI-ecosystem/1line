What it does

Instruction precision, not AI generation.

Most people know roughly what they want AI to do — but their instructions are too vague, too broad or too unstructured to get useful results. The problem is not the AI; it's the prompt.

1LINE AI-M takes one line describing your objective and transforms it into structured, refined prompts ready for any major AI model — compressed, role-framed and execution-ready. Deterministic refinement, not AI generation.
