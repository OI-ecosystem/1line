What it does

Stop rambling. Start landing.

Most people can do what they do brilliantly — but can't explain it. The description rambles, shifts depending on who's asking, and never quite lands the way it should.

1LINE Pitch takes one honest paragraph about what you do and transforms it into sharp, confident positioning: an elevator pitch, a written pitch, a one-liner, founder type, signal strength analysis and a positioning weakness to fix. Everything derived from your exact words.
