What it does

Symptoms, separated from the core.

People usually describe the symptoms, not the actual problem — the surface noise of a situation rather than what's really driving it. That's why thinking in circles rarely resolves anything.

1LINE Clarity reads your messy paragraph and separates noise from signal: it surfaces the core issue, what you actually want, and what's quietly blocking you — turning scattered thought into structured understanding.

Example input

“I keep overthinking every possible outcome before I act, and it feels like my mind is constantly split between wanting a different life and being afraid to leave the safety of what I already know, my mind is locked as I don't know which direction to go.”
