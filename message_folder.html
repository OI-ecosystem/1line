What it does

Same meaning. Clearer voice.

Some messages are hard to word — a text, an email, a request or reply you keep rewriting and still can't get right. The intent is clear in your head; the wording fights you.

1LINE Message rewrites your communication into clearer, calmer, more effective versions while preserving exactly what you mean. It reads the emotional temperature, tone and weak points in your draft, then returns versions tuned for different audiences and situations — professional, personal, direct or softened.

Example input

“I have been trying to explain how overwhelmed and frustrated I am lately, but every time I start the conversation it either comes out too emotional or too defensive and I end up saying things worse than I mean them.”
